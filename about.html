About Newsletter Maker

Run the script by selecting Make Newsletter. In the sidebar enter the email recipient. Usage is intended for an email to a google group, or to one proofreader who can then forward along. It does not currently handly multiple addresses.

Enter the subject line to accompany mail and click Send Email. This will pull content and translate from all the columns.

Script does not check to see if all subjects are there, it only takes columns and creates an html email, with a spanish translation.

Newsletter Maker was made at the Meaning Maker Studio, Alameda County Office of Education
